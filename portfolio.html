Our Portfolio.

At Capulet Property Investment we have a wide variety of projects that we carry out on a daily basis. Our refurbishment teams consist of fully qualified tradesmen. We don’t use un trained sub-contractors. Michael Massey the Operations Director personally oversees every project from start to finish.

Prior to any work been taken on Michael will discuss with you in great detail of what work will be carried out and when. You will receive a weekly update with a full breakdown of the works that have been completed. This report will be backed up by photographic evidence of the works being carried out. In addition to the report you will receive a planned schedule of works of what will commence the following week. As most of our clients are based outside of the North East, this gives our clients peace of mind in knowing what is being carried out on a weekly basis.

Our projects rage from minor works up to conversion projects and new developments with a GDV (Gross Development Value) of circa £2m. At Capulet Property Investment we believe that experience is key and it has taken us years and years to build up our core teams that produce high standards of work day in day out for our customers.
