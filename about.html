About Us

Michael Massey

Michael Massey is a third generation landlord. His family have been investing in properties in the North East since the 1950s. He has a BSc (Hons) degree in Estate Management and is currently undertaking his Senior RICS training. Michael is the Operations Director. His role is to oversee the external staff of the company. He looks after the buying team, personally checking every property before they get presented to clients. He also looks after the tradesmen on site carrying out all the refurbishments, as let’s face it someone has too!
